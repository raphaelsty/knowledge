Privacy Notice

What we collect

This site collects anonymous usage statistics to understand how the search engine is used and improve it. Under the CNIL audience measurement exemption, no consent banner is required because we only collect:

  • Page views — that you visited the site, with the referring domain (e.g. "google.com", not the full URL)
  • Search queries — what you searched for, result count, and response time
  • Clicks — which document you clicked (once per URL per session)
  • Filter & folder interactions — which source filters and folders you used
  • Device type — mobile or desktop (not your browser or OS)

What we do NOT collect

  • No IP addresses are stored
  • No cookies — only a random session ID in sessionStorage (cleared when you close the tab)
  • No cross-site tracking
  • No personal data
  • No fingerprinting (browser, OS, screen size, etc.)

Data retention

Events are automatically deleted after 90 days.

Third parties

Analytics data is not shared with any third party. It is stored on our own server.

Opt out

You can disable all analytics collection. This preference is saved in your browser and persists across sessions.
